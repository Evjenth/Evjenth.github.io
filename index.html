A STUDY OF ROV MOTIONS INDUCED BY MANIPULATOR ARMS:

A CASE STUDY OF THE USE OF MOVING FRAMES AND SE(3)

As a bachelor project, Andreas Evjenth, Otto Andreas Moe and Iselin Violet Kjelland Schøn have studied and analysed the induced motions on an ROV due to movement of its manipulator arms. With help and guidance from Professor Thomas J. Impelluso, an IMECE-article and this web-page with a 3D-web animation has also been produced.
