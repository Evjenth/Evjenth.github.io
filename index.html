Skipper

June 7, 2017

Skipper (båtførerprøven in norwegian) is a new app available in Google Play. It is a quiz-styled app that is meant to be a free and easy way to practice to the test which is required for anyone who is driving a boat over 8 meters of length in Norway. Download it in Google Play

Evjenth Apps

Evjenth Apps is a company developed by Andreas Evjenth. It's primary mission is to make simple, free android applications. The focus is on educational applications. Evjenth Apps has a vision that information and education should be free for the general public.
